About Us

Global Connect is designed to provide international students with the resources they need to succeed in their academic and personal lives. From finding jobs to maintaining social wellbeing, we cover it all.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a supportive community where international students can access vital information and connect with peers. We believe in fostering an inclusive environment that celebrates diversity and promotes academic excellence.

Services We Offer

We offer a wide range of services including:

  • International Student Advice
  • Job Listings
  • Social Wellbeing Programs
  • Fun Activities and Events
  • Funding Opportunities
